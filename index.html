About

An awesome fps-rts game created by some inexperienced high-school dudes. Right now, run and shoot each other. And by "each other", I mean some white pills that don't resemble any life form whatsoever. There are four awesome maps currently, with more on the way. Chatting is being implemented so you can tell the other players how big of scubs they are.

Us four are slaving away, making this game so you better love it. I'm serious. We will never talk to you again if you dislike it.
